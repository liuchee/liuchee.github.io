Short Bio

[News]: I have joined Megvii Technology (Face++), as Research Lead in Megvii Research Chengdu.

[News]：We are looking for both full time researchers and interns, in vision, deep learning, graphics and related areas.
"Power human with AI", if you happen to think the same and want to join us at Megvii Research Chengdu, please send your resume to liushuaicheng@megvii.com

Shuaicheng Liu received his Ph.D degree at Electrical & Computer Engineering, supervised by Prof. Ping Tan in 2014 and received his Master degree at School of Computing,supervised by Prof. Michael S.Brown, in 2010, both from National University of Singapore. He obtained his Bachelor degree in Sichuan University in 2008. He joined the group of Prof. Bing Zeng at School of Electronic Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2014. During the internship, he was luckily mentored by Dr. Jian Sun, Dr. Lu Yuan at Microsoft Research and Dr. Jue Wang, Dr. Sunghyun Cho at Adobe. His research interest includes computer vision and computer graphics.
